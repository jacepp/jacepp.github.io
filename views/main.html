How to play

  • Choose a username and click "Jump In"
  • If there are at least two players, a Chuck Norris joke will be displayed in a light gray text.
  • As you type in the provided text area, the letters you correctly type will be colored green. If you mis-type something, from the letter you made the mistake and on will be turned red until corrected.
  • You will be able to see the progress of the player who types the fastest (unless it's you). They're position in the joke will be the white letter with a dark green background.
  • Once you or the lead player finish typing the joke, a new one will appear within a couple seconds.
  • To the right of the screen there is a list of players with their wins and losses. You will always be the top person in this list.
  • Beneath the text area, there is a player leaderboard. It will display the players with the most wins, losses, or longest winning streak.
  • Keep in mind that player's stats are based on socket sessions, this means if you leave or refresh the page, your stats will be reset.
  • Most important of all, have fun.
